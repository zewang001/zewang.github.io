  • Art Style Transfer with Convolutional Neural

    Transform photos with an extracted style from a painting with CNN, e.g., Van Gogh’s Starry Night. The purpose of the project is to manipulate the intermediate output from separate layers, instead of using only the final labelling. Pre-trained CNN model VGG19 is used for content and style extraction. The solution extracts the content of photo from deeper layer and the style of painting from output of several layers. Gram matrix is used to calculate the correlations of the outputs of layers to generate the style. The extracted content and style are added together to get the art-styled photo.
